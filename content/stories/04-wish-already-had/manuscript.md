A child stands at the mouth of a bright, humming jungle beside a quiet friend, peeks past an arch of morning glories, and takes the first brave step into the green. Page by page the jungle opens itself up — a frog who hums his own tiny theme song, a toucan whose beak clicks like castanets, a butterfly who trails glitter, a sloth whose smile arrives so slowly it sneaks up on you — and curiosity blooms into full laughing wonder. By the time {HERO} and {COMPANION_NAME} reach the sparkly pool at the jungle's hidden heart, adventure has stopped being a word and become a warm feeling they get to carry home in their pockets.
