Produce a single SETTING REFERENCE SHEET illustration in vibrant watercolor style for a children's picture book. This image will anchor every page of a zoo-loop walk where the pair meets one animal at a time along the path — it is the visual "likeness" of the connecting pathway and its multiple habitat edges.

NO human characters. NO animals. Just the environment and its recurring props, clearly visible. (Only tiny ambient cameos allowed: a distant butterfly, a dragonfly skimming the pond, or a background sparrow — no more, and no zoo animals.)

Scene: A gently winding zoo pathway that loops past several habitat edges, viewed in warm bright midday sun, three-quarter angle that shows the sandy giraffe yard edge to the back-left, the lily-pad pond in the middle of the path, the leafy jungle edge to the far-right, and the picnic bench tucked along the side.

- A single white PICNIC BENCH tucked along the path under a yellow shade UMBRELLA, warm cheerful yellow panels

Environment details (soft, lived-in):

Art style: modern vibrant watercolor illustration. Rich saturated colors, confident playful shapes, soft paper grain, contemporary picture-book energy — think Oliver Jeffers, Sam Usher, Christian Robinson at their most vivid. NOT muted, NOT vintage, NOT sepia. Bright and joyful. Soft edges, painterly, no harsh black outlines.

Composition: wide horizontal framing, lily-pad pond as the clear middle visual anchor, giraffe yard edge with acacia background-left, jungle edge far-right, signpost at the fork just beyond the pond, picnic bench with yellow umbrella tucked along the side, wildflower patches edging the cobbles, wood-and-rope fence running the length of the path. Exclusions (critical): no text, no letters, no numbers, no readable words on any sign (pictograms only), no labels, no watermarks, no signatures, no borders, no frames, no panels, no humans, no visitors, no crowds, no giraffe, flamingo, sloth, parrot, or zebra rendered here (those are moving characters on later pages), no night.
