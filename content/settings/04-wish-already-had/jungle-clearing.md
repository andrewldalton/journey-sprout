Produce a single SETTING REFERENCE SHEET illustration in vibrant watercolor style for a children's picture book. This image will anchor every page of a hidden jungle clearing adventure — it is the visual "likeness" of the location.

NO human characters. NO animals. NO story creatures (no humming frog, no toucan, no glitter butterfly, no slow sloth — those arrive on later pages). Just the environment and its recurring props, clearly visible. (Only tiny ambient cameos allowed: a drifting pollen mote or a single fallen leaf on the water — no more.)

Scene: A tucked-away hidden jungle clearing, viewed in warm mid-morning green-gold light, three-quarter angle that shows the sparkly pool at center, the waterfall ribboning down at back-left, the fallen mossy log lower-right, and the arching fern canopy overhead.

Required architectural elements (render clearly and memorably):

- A flowering BROMELIAD PATCH along the BACK-RIGHT, tall spiky leaves with bright red and orange bloom-spikes rising cheerfully from their centers
- An arching FERN CANOPY overhead, curving fronds of emerald and chartreuse fern framing the top of the frame
- Twin BANANA-LEAF PADDLES flanking the pool on either side, broad glossy green leaves leaning in from LEFT and RIGHT like natural curtains

Required recurring props (render each exactly once, in specific positions that will repeat across all pages):
- A single fallen jungle LEAF drifting on the pool surface, bright yellow-green

Environment details (soft, lived-in):

Lighting: narrow slanting SUNBEAMS piercing through the canopy from the upper-right at roughly 4300K, warm green-gold filtered through ferns, landing bright and warm directly on the POOL SURFACE as soft spotlights, deeper cooler green shade in the outer edges. Honey-bright at the center, mossy-cool at the edges.

Art style: modern vibrant watercolor illustration. Rich saturated colors, confident playful shapes, soft paper grain, contemporary picture-book energy — think Oliver Jeffers, Sam Usher, Christian Robinson at their most vivid. NOT muted, NOT vintage, NOT sepia. Bright and joyful. Soft edges, painterly, no harsh black outlines.

Composition: wide horizontal framing, sparkly pool as the clear center visual anchor, waterfall back-left as the vertical source, fallen mossy log lower-right as foreground anchor, bromeliad patch back-right for warm color punch, twin banana-leaf paddles flanking the pool, arching fern canopy cradling the top of the frame, narrow sun-shafts dropping onto the pool. Plenty of open pool-edge so future page illustrations have room for two small explorers and their quiet creature friends on this same anchored stage.

Exclusions (critical): no text, no letters, no numbers, no labels, no watermarks, no signatures, no borders, no frames, no panels, no humans, no child, no companion creature, no frog, no toucan, no butterfly, no sloth, no predators, no night, no rain.
