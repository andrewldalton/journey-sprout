NO human characters. NO animals other than tiny background minnows. Just the environment and its recurring props, clearly visible.

Scene: A sunlit shallow coral reef just beneath the surface of the sea, viewed in bright midday light, three-quarter angle that shows the reef floor, the middle-water column, and the wavy surface of the water at the very top of the frame with light breaking through.

- A narrow ribbon of pale golden SAND BEACH visible at the very top edge of the frame where the water meets a small tide-pool shore — a single bleached DRIFTWOOD LOG and three small TIDE POOLS in the rocks are hinted at

- A single soft-pink anemone peeking out from behind the brain coral

Lighting: bright midday sun from upper-right at roughly 5200K filtered through clear blue shallow water, clean turquoise-gold warmth, long soft caustics stretching across the sand, warm and unworried.

Art style: warm watercolor with digital polish, soft edges, visible paper grain, rich teals and warm golds and coral pinks, soft blurred middle water with sharper foreground coral and props, no harsh black outlines, no line-art. Painterly and inviting.

Composition: wide horizontal framing, brain coral as the clear visual anchor, sea fan and pink archway balanced around it, sea grass low-right, sunbeams slanting from upper-right, a soft turquoise water column rising to a barely visible wavy surface at the very top. Exclusions (critical): no text, no letters, no numbers, no labels, no watermarks, no signatures, no borders, no frames, no panels, no humans, no large sea animals (no whales, no sharks, no dolphins, no octopuses — those appear on later pages as moving characters, not in the setting sheet).
