Produce a single SETTING REFERENCE SHEET illustration in warm watercolor style for a children's picture book. This image will anchor every page of the climax sequence of an underwater adventure story, set inside a glowing underwater coral-hollow garden — it is the visual "likeness" of the location.

NO human characters. NO large animals. Just the environment and its recurring props, clearly visible.

Scene: A rounded coral HOLLOW deep below the surface of the sea, viewed in soft gentle glow, three-quarter angle that shows the sandy floor, the coral walls ringing the hollow, and the domed coral ceiling arching overhead. It feels like a room made of coral — a secret garden you could step into.

Required environment elements (render clearly and memorably):
- A rounded domed ceiling of warm pink-and-cream CORAL arching overhead, textured with soft bumps and dapples, catching faint light
- A ring of soft pastel ANEMONES (lilac, peach, sea-foam, rose) around the hollow's walls, each open like a slow flower with gently waving fronds — the story's soft "petal" motif
- Tall strands of pale blue-green LIGHT KELP rising from behind the anemones, glowing faintly from within like lit glass
- A flat sandy CLEARING at the hollow's floor, smooth and soft, the central visual stage for the scene
- Scattered GLOWING PEARLS tucked into the coral walls like tiny stars, each a warm soft-white glow — some small, a few medium
- Gentle beams of soft turquoise LIGHT filtering down through a small opening in the ceiling, giving the hollow a hushed magical quality
- A scatter of tiny rising BUBBLES drifting slowly upward in two places, suggesting gentle current
- A single larger FRIENDLY PEARL the size of a small apple, resting on the sandy clearing at center as the visual anchor object

Required recurring props (render each exactly once, in specific positions that will repeat across all pages):
- A soft cushion-shape of silvery MOSS at the base of the kelp on the left, where the grandmother sea turtle might naturally rest (but she is NOT in this sheet)
- A flat piece of smooth MOTHER-OF-PEARL SHELL the size of a dinner plate on the sand just right of center, iridescent in soft pinks and blues, catching light
- A small cluster of three pale SEA-STARS on one of the coral walls, arranged like a friendly trio

Environment details (soft, lived-in):
- Gentle floating specks of plankton drifting in the light
- A faint shimmer in the water like a breeze just touched it
- A small hidden glow deeper inside one of the anemones

Lighting: soft gentle filtered glow, cool turquoise with warm gold highlights from the glowing pearls and light-kelp, hushed and magical, evening-of-the-sea quality. Not dark — softly luminous. Warm, hopeful, welcoming.

Art style: warm watercolor with digital polish, soft edges, visible paper grain, a palette of soft pinks, pearly creams, lilac, sea-foam, and gentle turquoise, with warm golden glow accents, soft blurred middle space with sharper foreground coral and props, no harsh black outlines, no line-art. Painterly and inviting.

Composition: wide horizontal framing, the central sandy clearing as the clear visual stage, anemone ring embracing the space, kelp rising behind, domed coral ceiling arching above, glowing pearls scattered like stars in the walls, the anchor pearl at center, mother-of-pearl shell right of center. Plenty of open sandy floor so future page illustrations have room for action to take place on this same anchored stage.

Exclusions (critical): no text, no letters, no numbers, no labels, no watermarks, no signatures, no borders, no frames, no panels, no humans, no animals (the grandmother sea turtle, fish, octopus, etc. appear as moving characters on later pages — not in the setting sheet).
